The Ghost Thread Lifecycle

How our innovative fishing line safely returns to nature

YEAR 0: First Use

Ghost Thread performs identically to premium nylon fishing line, with all the same sensitivity, strength and castability that anglers expect.

YEAR 1-3: Saltwater Activation

After exposure to saltwater, the proprietary biodegradable coating begins breaking down. The line remains fully functional throughout this period.

YEAR 4-5: Structural Breakdown

Material begins fragmenting into smaller pieces that marine organisms can naturally process. Tensile strength gradually decreases, preventing accidental entanglement.

YEAR 6-7: Complete Biodegradation

Microorganisms fully metabolize the line into harmless organic compounds that become part of the natural marine nutrient cycle, with no toxic byproducts.
